About Floki

Floki is a name from Norse mythology. He is the son of Loki. Which means that Floki is the Prince of Mischief. Hence his lil nickname as the Prince of Mischief...and just like his namesake, he is definitely a Prince of Mischief. But all I know is...

  • He is a brat but we love him
  • Our lives will never be the same
  • Many more adventures to come

There are so many things I could say about this pet, but I do have others...click the heart to see their photos.

Sometimes I wonder why do I love this animal?

But then...I think of all the ways my life is different.

  • 01 Would you have fabulous walks without him?

    This simple answer is no.

  • 02 Would you have new shoes now?

    No, but I would still have my old ones.

  • 03 Would you smile every day?

    Probably, but not as genuinely just by looking at his adorable little face and his fuzzy tail.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions we receive about owning a Floki.

  • How old is he?

    Old enough to reach things he shouldn't but not old enough to know better.

  • Is that a wolf?

    Maybe....No he is probably not a wolf.

  • What kind of dog is that then?

    He is a mix between a Siberian Husky and a Golden Retriever.

  • Is he friendly?

    Does a bear poop in the woods? But he is quite energetic and a bit aggressive-friendly...

  • Does he know any tricks?

    He knows several: how to steal shoes, how to steal food, how to find hidden treasures, how to dig a den, how to wrangle cats, how to chase bugs and birds.
